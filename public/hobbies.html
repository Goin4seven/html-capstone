  • Play Video Games

    • My current favorite video game to play is Escape From Tarkov. This game provides an interesting take on surviving in an active warzone. Every item you loot throughout the game is important. Each item you pick up can be used for something. There are food and drink items that you will need to prevent you from starving or becoming dehydrated. There are items that can be used for crafting and some items need to be found for quests. As you progress through the game you encounter enemies of all kinds, including the environment.
